Welcome to the EDAM Control Room

The EDAM ontology

EDAM is an ontology of well-established concepts relevant to life sciences, bioinformatics analyses and data management. EDAM includes 4 sub-ontologies: topics, operations, types of data and data formats, and defines their relations with one another.

The purpose of the control room is to facilitate collaborative efforts towards the improvement of the EDAM ontology, by identifying gaps in the current version and providing an interface for their easy integration.
